Designers and coordination Team: Address the needs and the functioning of the community where the vermicomposter will be installed. High outreach and educational involvement during the initial phases.

Testing Community: Take responsibility for the care of the vermicomposter and the worm community inside it. Responsibilities include:
- Periodic maintenance of the humus and replacement of soil.
- Extraction of the leachate.
- Monitoring the worm community and its population growth or decline.
- Adjusting the diet according to smell, season, humidity, etc.
