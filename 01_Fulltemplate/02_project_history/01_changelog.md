# **Vermicomposter v 1.0:**

Copenhagen - January 2025

Reusing materials of Maker Association Workshop (wood + plastic screw boxes).

Reason: Although there was an existing concern for recycling, there was no protocol for reusing the organic waste generated daily in V-10.

Design based in the set of furniture previously fabricated in V-10.
Responsible: Designers Team.

# **Vermicomposter v 2.0:**

Sevilla - March 2025

Vida XL commercial vermicomposter system + mashrabiya shell.

Reason: Dimensioning adjusted to the needs of the worm community. Emphasis on learning and protection practices around worm care and humus management. Experimental work with humus.

Changes: Main dimensions based in worms morphological requirements (greater horizontal surface and limited depth), integrated in proved vertical system.

Design based in the Gardens in the Air mashrabiya system, adaptable for vertical and horizontal installations.
Responsible: Designers Team.

# **Vermicomposter v 2.1:**

Sevilla - July 2025

Ceramic pots hacked with 3d prints + mashrabiya shell.

Reason: Enhanced resilience to extreme temperatures, enabling evapotranspirative cooling. Improved aesthetic integration of the vermicomposting unit.

Changes: Improved outdoor performance, self-produced design independent of commercial systems, and adaptation to local resources and operational models through parametric design.

Design based in Traditional Ceramic Pot with a hacked lid and stackable trays integrated in the Gardens in the Air mashrabiya system.
Responsible: Designers Team.
